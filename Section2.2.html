SECTION 2 MATERIALS, WORKMANSHIP, INSPECTION AND TESTING

2.1 MATERIALS

All materials shall conform to the relevant standards specified herein. Materials shall be free from defects and shall be of the quality specified. Certificates of compliance shall be provided for all critical materials.

Storage of materials shall prevent deterioration and contamination. Materials shall be clearly identified and segregated according to their specifications.

2.2 WORKMANSHIP

Workmanship shall comply with industry best practices and approved procedures. All personnel shall be qualified for their assigned tasks and possess current certifications.

Work areas shall be maintained in a clean and orderly condition. All operations shall be performed under appropriate environmental conditions.
